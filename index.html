Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 5, 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Plain Language Summary

  • Your location is used only to fetch weather for that location. It is never stored on our servers.
  • We do not sell your data. Ever. To anyone.
  • We do not use your data for advertising.
  • The barometer sensor is read on-device only. No pressure readings leave your phone.
  • Analytics are aggregate and anonymous — used only to improve forecast accuracy.
  • You can use Wexer without creating an account.

1.Who We Are

Wexer is a hyperlocal weather application developed and operated as an independent app. References to "Wexer," "we," "us," or "our" in this policy refer to the Wexer app and its developer.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Wexer accesses, how it is used, and your rights with respect to that information.

2.Information We Access

Wexer accesses the following types of information from your device:

Data Type Purpose Stored?
GPS Location (precise) Fetch hyperlocal weather for your exact position Never
Barometric Pressure (sensor) Real-time pressure trend alerts on-device Never
Device Speed & Altitude (GPS) Driving detection to suppress false pressure alerts Never
Searched Locations Display weather for user-chosen locations Never
Anonymous Usage Analytics Improve forecast accuracy and app performance Aggregate only

3.How We Use Your Information

Information accessed by Wexer is used exclusively to provide weather services to you. Specifically:

  • Your GPS coordinates are sent to third-party weather APIs (Open-Meteo, NOAA, Synoptic Data) to retrieve forecast and observation data. These requests contain only coordinates — no identifiers.
  • Barometric pressure readings from your phone's sensor are processed entirely on-device. No pressure data is transmitted anywhere.
  • Location search queries are sent to OpenStreetMap Nominatim for geocoding. Queries contain only the text you type.
  • Anonymous aggregate analytics (e.g., which features are used, forecast accuracy by region) are collected to improve the app. These contain no personal identifiers.

4.What We Never Do

  • We never sell your personal data to any third party.
  • We never share your location with advertisers.
  • We never build a profile of your movements, habits, or behavior.
  • We never use your data for targeted advertising of any kind.
  • We never store your precise GPS coordinates on any server.
  • We never access your contacts, camera, microphone, or any other device feature beyond those listed in Section 2.

5.Third-Party Weather Data Services

Wexer uses the following third-party APIs to provide weather data. Your approximate coordinates are sent to these services to retrieve weather information. Each service has its own privacy policy.

  • Open-Meteo — open-meteo.com — Weather forecasts and current conditions
  • NOAA / NWS — weather.gov — Official US weather observations and alerts
  • Synoptic Data — synopticdata.com — Real-time weather station observations
  • Tomorrow.io — tomorrow.io — Weather data validation
  • RainViewer — rainviewer.com — Radar imagery
  • FAA Aviation Weather Center — aviationweather.gov — METAR observations
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — nominatim.openstreetmap.org — Location geocoding
  • Mapbox — mapbox.com — Map tiles

None of these services receive any personally identifying information from Wexer beyond the coordinates necessary to retrieve weather data.

6.Location Permissions

Wexer requests access to your device's precise location to display hyperlocal weather conditions. This permission is optional — if denied, Wexer will use a default location.

Location is accessed in the foreground only when the app is open. Wexer does not request background location access.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device's Settings app without affecting other Wexer features.

7.Barometer & Device Sensors

With your permission, Wexer reads the barometric pressure sensor built into your device. This data is used exclusively on-device to:

  • Detect rapid pressure drops that may indicate approaching severe weather
  • Generate local pressure trend alerts
  • Detect driving conditions to suppress false altitude-induced alerts

Barometric sensor data is never transmitted off your device. It is processed in memory and discarded when you close the app.

8.Children's Privacy

Wexer is suitable for all ages and is rated 4+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since Wexer does not collect personal information from any users, no special collection relating to children occurs.

9.Data Security

Because Wexer does not store personal data on our servers, the primary security considerations are on-device. All API requests use HTTPS encrypted connections. Sensor data never leaves your device.

10.Your Rights

Because Wexer does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to request, correct, or delete. You may:

  • Revoke location permission at any time via device Settings
  • Disable barometer access at any time via device Settings
  • Use Wexer entirely without granting any permissions by entering locations manually

If you are in the European Union, California, or another jurisdiction with specific data rights laws, you may contact us at the address below with any questions. Given that we do not store personal data, most data rights requests will be satisfied by the fact that there is no data to act upon.

11.Changes to This Policy
